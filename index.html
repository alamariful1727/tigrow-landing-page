What we do ?

An AI based social network for professionals, that provides learning, marketplace, recruitment and intra-company communication in one place.

TiGrow Learning Centre

Facilitates veteran professional leaders to share their knowledge.
Introducing Mentor & Educator.
Mentors will select and groom a small and exclusive group of individual or companies with their professional insights.
Educators will share their knowledge to a much larger audience, through video series or presentations on relevant topics.

TiGrow Recruitment Centre

Recruiting is made much more easier with Tigrow. Find potential partners for Businesses. Add one’s experience and educational summary and skill test results in the mix to find the best candidate. In TiGrow Community, People who want to partner with other people to start businesses can easily find each other. Through the Introduction Video feature, get a peak into a candidates personality.
